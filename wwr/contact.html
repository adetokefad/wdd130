DOWN UNDER RAFTING

Indulge in the most thrilling outdoor experience we have to offer with our multi-day, daring white water rafting excursions. At a leisurely pace, take in the breathtaking splendor of the river canyon and relish each minute. We personally attend to every aspect of your day to make sure you have an absolutely wonderful experience with us.

HISTORY

The history of white water rafting is extensive and goes all the way back to ancient times. From a useful mode of transportation, it has developed into a thrilling pastime that many people love. In the 1970s, it became a popular adventure activity, which prompted more safety precautions and attention to environmental preservation.
